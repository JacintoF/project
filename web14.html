Online Space is a Complete Construct

There is no such thing.

Programmers have viewers scroll down a site because it makes sense to the human brain. Like when driving a car, one continuous path in the same direction feels like progress, it connects with people's daily experience. But since online space is all molded by program there is no reason the navigation of virtual space must be the same as space in the physical world. Instead the rules can change on a whim. Now new pages are at the top and scrolling down will reveal old content instead of new pages.
